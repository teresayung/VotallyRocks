About

Votally is a mobile application that facilitates photo based polling amongst friends. Just take a picture, write a comment, and send it to your friends. Your friends will receive it and tell you what they think!

Whether you're out shopping wondering whether or not you should get that dress, walking in the streets wondering who wants to see Big Hero 6 tonight, Votally is the perfect solution for you to get fast, easy results from your friends!
